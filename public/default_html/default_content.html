Historical Data This is a repository of significant historical world events. These events caused the formation of alliances, treaties and trade agreements among countries. These inter-country relations correspond directly to inter-country business and trade contracts. This information gives insight into global business trends.
Current Streaming Data This is a real-time data feed. Actual opinions, perspectives and outlooks are obtained from social media data collections. The information is subject to appropriateness and moderation rules that are in place at these websites. Certain weblog feeds are also used as a source of information. Business news feeds are grouped separately. This information gives insight into emerging markets and innovations.
Future Predictive Information This is collated information. This collection contains analytic summaries prooduced by Investor analysts who research historical data , historical business trends and model streaming data to predict future business trends. These summaries are grouped according to special areas where business growth and progress is possible. Areas that are depreciating are also listed.
